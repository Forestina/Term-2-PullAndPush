MYSTERIOUS DAILY LIFE

Mei Zhang & Jiaxu Wu


Oh, man!

What did you do last night?

Playing cell phone, over and over again

Put it down and pick it up

Stay up late


The lamp once out

Cool stars enter

The window frame.


I lay in bed, with a racing mind

Thoughts swirling, hard to unwind

The phone still calling, tempting me

But I knew I needed to let it be


So I gazed out into the night

And saw the stars shining bright

Their peaceful glow a calming sight

And slowly, my mind took flight


Away from screens and constant noise

Towards a world of inner poise

Where the mind can rest and rejuvenate

And find the peace that we all crave


So I closed my eyes and breathed deep

And let my worries drift to sleep

And with the stars as my guide

I found the calm I needed inside.
